ABOUT ME

I’m a Full-Stack Web Developer

There are two things I have loved as a little kid: Arts and Math. Math equations were rewarding because there always was and will be an answer despite its difficulty. In contrast, Arts allowed me to tangibly express my thoughts and imaginations.

As a Web Developer, I could do the two things I loved at the same time.

To me, coding is not difficult, but rather, challenging because if I put in the time and efforts, I will see the result. Every project and experience I encounter build me up to be a stronger developer ready to take on new challenges. I firmly believe that the skills, ideas, and creativity I have will spark a large impact on those around me.

Exploring hidden cafés while watching videos of BTS,
Celebrating Friday night with fresh, cold beer,
Playing with my angelic, five-year-old golden retriever,
and I am an avid traveler! Feel free to ask me about my experiences!
